# Privacy Policy — Tone

_Last updated: June 20, 2026_

Tone is designed to collect nothing.

## No data collection
Tone does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

## Microphone
Tone uses the microphone only to detect the pitch of the sound around it. Audio is processed entirely on your device, in real time, to estimate a musical note. Audio is never recorded, saved, or sent off the device.

## Fully offline
Tone makes no network connections and works with no internet access.

## Third parties
Tone contains no third-party advertising or analytics code. Its only third-party components are open-source audio libraries (AudioKit / SoundpipeAudioKit) that run on-device and send nothing anywhere.

## Children
Tone collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page.
