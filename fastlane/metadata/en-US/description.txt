Tone is a chromatic tuner that does one thing well: tell you the pitch — clearly and instantly.

No ads. No accounts. No sign-up. No analytics. Open it and play.

WHAT IT DOES
• Hears any note from your instrument and shows the note, octave, and how many cents sharp or flat you are.
• Uses a calm linear scale instead of a jittery needle, so the reading is easy to trust.
• Turns a single accent color the moment you're in tune, with a gentle haptic tap — keep your eyes on the instrument, not the screen.
• Adjustable reference pitch: A4 from 415 to 466 Hz.
• Reference tone (tuning fork): switch to FORK mode, pick any note and octave, and play a clean sustained tone to tune by ear — no microphone needed.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, saved, or sent anywhere. Tone works fully offline and contains no third-party tracking.

DESIGNED FOR iOS
A native Liquid Glass interface with VoiceOver and Dynamic Type support.

Tune a guitar, violin, cello, wind instrument, or anything with a clear pitch.
